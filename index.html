About the Villa

A place to rest close to nature.

Nusari Villa blends modern minimalist architecture with Ubud's tropical landscape. From the pool deck, balcony, and living room, every space is made to feel calm and open for a staycation, a small family trip, or a slower work-from-villa rhythm.

Located in Mas, a cultural village near Ubud's attractions, the villa remains tucked away enough to feel private from the moment you arrive.
